# oasis-infobyte-data-science-task-5
---
Sales Prediction
---
This project was developed during my Data Science internship at Oasis Infobyte. It analyzes the relationship between marketing strategies and sales revenue to support data-driven business decisions.

---

📌 Project Objective
To forecast future sales performance by modeling the correlation between advertising expenditure across various marketing channels (TV, Radio, Newspaper) and total revenue.

---

🛠️ Key Steps Followed

Correlation Analysis: Conducted a deep dive into the relationship between marketing budgets and sales outcomes to identify the most effective channels.

Data Visualization: Created trend charts and scatter plots to map sales performance trajectories relative to advertising spend.

Predictive Modeling: Built a regression model to estimate future sales figures based on historical budget allocation data.

Strategic Insights: Interpreted model coefficients to provide actionable recommendations for optimizing future marketing budget distribution.

---

💻 Technologies Used

Language: Python

Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit-learn

Development Environment: VS Code with Jupyter Notebooks
